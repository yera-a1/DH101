Week 5 Reflection

Prompt: How does AI alter authorship and remix culture? Who owns AI-made art?

Journal Response

Here is an example of where AI can overstep human autonomy. Having an AI generate pictures of specific cultural phenomenon can only lead to confusion, as the AI is not human. It has not experienced such things firsthand, and is not able to recreate accurately such things, or rather it would not be able to generate something that is true to its nature. This raises critical questions about authenticity and cultural representation in AI-generated work, highlighting the importance of human oversight and cultural knowledge in the creative process.
