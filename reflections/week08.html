Week 8 Reflection

Prompt: Who is visible/invisible in AI networks? How does visualization reveal or obscure power?

Journal Response

Here is an example of AI being used as a tool. Here, we see a collection of data that would otherwise be difficult to organize. But with AI, we are able to easily make sense of the nonsensical. But also note how the brunt of the work was done by a human, through research and data collection, and how AI acted only as the finishing touch. Here, AI is in its best element. When humans drive the creative and analytical process—gathering data, making decisions, and guiding the outcome—AI becomes a powerful finishing tool that amplifies human capability rather than replacing human effort.
