Image Alt Text Guide

This page provides detailed descriptions of all images used in the DH101 Critical Making project. These descriptions serve as comprehensive alt text for accessibility purposes.

Week 3: Selfie & Identity

Image 1: First AI-Generated Selfie

Alt Text: First AI-generated selfie showing a young African American male with afro hairstyle in studio lighting

Description: An AI-generated portrait of an 18-year-old African American male created using DALL·E. The image shows the subject with an approximately 4-inch afro with a low taper fade, straightened teeth, and is photographed in studio lighting conditions. The subject is wearing what appears to be casual clothing.

Image 2: Second AI-Generated Selfie (Revised)

Alt Text: Second AI-generated selfie with refined features including larger muscles, cleaner hair, studio lighting, and streetwear clothing

Description: An AI-generated portrait that is a refined version of the first image. This version features requested modifications including slightly larger muscles, cleaner/more refined hair styling, professional studio lighting, and casual streetwear clothing such as baggy jeans. This represents the iterative refinement process of AI image generation based on user feedback.

Week 4: Comic & Storytelling

Racial Bias in AI-Generated Dialogue Comic

Alt Text: Comic demonstrating racial bias in AI-generated dialogue with multiple panels showing dialogue between characters

Description: A multi-panel comic artwork created to demonstrate how AI encoding bias into dialogue generation. The comic shows two characters in different scenarios with AI-generated dialogue. The dialogue reflects different dialects and speech patterns that the creator prompted the AI to generate based on character race. This visual work illustrates how easily human biases can be embedded into AI systems and reproduced in generated content.

Week 5: GIF & Remix Culture

Facial Expression Progression GIF Animation

Alt Text: Animated GIF showing facial expression progression from open eyes with blank expression to closed eyes with smile

Description: A 12-frame animated GIF created using EZGIF showing the progression of a person's facial expression. The animation starts with the subject's eyes open and a neutral/blank expression, gradually transitioning through various expressions, and ending with eyes closed and a smile. This animation references the "Ishowspeed meme" from popular culture, demonstrating AI's ability (and limitations) in recreating culturally significant emotional expressions and memes. The GIF plays at 10 frames per second.

Week 6: Text & Distant Reading

Image 1: Voyant Text Analysis Visualization

Alt Text: Voyant text analysis visualization showing word frequency and distribution in The Raven by Edgar Allan Poe

Description: A screenshot from the Voyant text analysis tool displaying data visualization of word frequencies and patterns in Edgar Allan Poe's "The Raven." The visualization shows which words appear most frequently in the text and their distribution throughout the work, allowing for distant reading analysis of the poem.

Image 2: ChatGPT Analysis of The Raven

Alt Text: ChatGPT analysis and summary of The Raven showing AI-generated literary interpretation

Description: A screenshot displaying ChatGPT's response to an in-depth analysis request about Edgar Allan Poe's "The Raven." The AI provides a bulleted summary and interpretation of the poem's themes, structure, and literary significance. This image contrasts machine analysis with human interpretation of literature.

Image 3: Additional Text Analysis Visualization

Alt Text: Additional text analysis visualization showing different metrics and patterns from The Raven analysis

Description: A supplementary screenshot from the Voyant tool or similar analysis platform showing additional metrics, patterns, or different visualization types for the analysis of "The Raven." This image demonstrates multiple approaches to distant reading and quantitative literary analysis.

Week 7: Mapping AI Worlds

Data Center Locations Map for Meta and Amazon

Alt Text: Map showing data center locations for Meta and Amazon across the United States

Description: A custom Google My Maps visualization displaying the geographic distribution of data centers operated by Meta (Facebook) and Amazon across the United States. The map reveals a concentration of facilities in the Midwest and other regions that offer advantageous conditions such as lower energy costs, tax incentives, and cooler climates for server cooling. This visualization makes visible the hidden infrastructure and geographic dimensions of AI deployment and environmental impact.

Week 8: Networks of Knowledge & Power

AI Supply Chain Network Diagram

Alt Text: Network diagram created with Kumu showing AI supply chains and resources

Description: A network visualization created using the Kumu platform that maps the complex supply chains involved in AI production. The diagram shows the relationships between important resources (such as silicon, rare earth minerals), producing countries (particularly China), manufacturing facilities, and final AI product consumers. This visualization illustrates how labor and resource extraction in certain regions often remains invisible to end users, highlighting power dynamics and the hidden human costs of AI infrastructure.
