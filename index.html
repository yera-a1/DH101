Welcome

This site documents my critical making this semester.

Throughout these 13 weeks, I've explored the intersection of digital humanities, technology ethics, and creative inquiry through hands-on projects and reflective practice at Denison University in DH101.

Each week combines a Make (a documented project or artifact) with a Reflection (critical analysis and response to guiding questions). Together, these form a comprehensive record of my learning journey.

Explore the site: Check out the Weekly Makes to see my projects, read my Reflections for deeper thinking, and visit the More Info section for an overview of key themes and my approach to accessibility and AI use.
