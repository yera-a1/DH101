Welcome

Throughout the course of this class, I have come to have a better understanding of the intersection point between humanity and AI. Starting the semester, I saw AI as a crutch, something that could be used to cut corners and subvert human interaction and input. I thought that throughout the course of the class, I would continue to hold this conception. But over the many weeks, I have come to have a better understanding of AI as a whole. AI is not a crutch; it is a tool that can enhance the human creativity experience. It can help us to categorize raw data, fill in the gaps of our knowledge, get us starting ideas to create even larger ones, and overall improve human efficiency as a whole. The thing is, AI can only do this much when it has one thing. You. Without human input, AI can't write its own prompts, start with its own ideas, or create and organize its own factual data. AI needs a human to start all of these processes; it needs someone to utilize it as a tool to the fullest. This realization took the entire semester to arrive at, and without the course as a whole, I do not think that I would have ever seen AI through this lens. As you navigate through my website, you are not just flipping through my makes from week to week. You are taking a deep dive into my intellectual awakening, seeing every documentation in that process.
