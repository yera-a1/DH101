## Process Notes
Canva: Used for zine construction
The sources that I ended up using ranged from activist organizations to official news outlets. This mix gave me a view into the objective facts that surrounded the mining of these resources, as well as the subjective opinions of the people affected by these operations. When it came to my research, and what was hard to find, I would have to say that I actually did not have any difficulty finding anything. Rather, I want to actually touch on how easy it was to find this information. When looking up key figures in the allocation of these resources for A.I., everything was essentially spoon-fed to me without my requiring much digging. Figures of scale, profit, and key owners were all laid bare for me to see, and it made the construction of my zine all that much easier. This ease of access to information surprised me the most when I was researching. The reasons for this are due to two considerations that came to my mind. Firstly, if the information is so easy to find, why don’t more people know about and understand it? It takes only a search or two to see the effects of resource allocation in the places they are being mined, but for some reason, it is something that is not being commonly discussed. The second consideration that came to my mind was the lack of care of companies that are exploiting these resources and workers to hide their dealings. I think it speaks to the fact that they understand that people have very low consideration for how the final product that they are consuming is made, and the process that goes into it. This only works to their benefit, as they can continue to exploit these areas and their populations for cheap and affordable labor and materials.
