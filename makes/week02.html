Process Notes

This week our class set up our individual Github accounts, and learned overall about the platform as a whole. This is our real first step into understanding AI as a tool, and doing it for ourselves.

Reflection

I think AI overall leads to many patterns. Many AI models are being made today, from ChatGPT to OpenAI, all offering different versions of the same non-differentiated product. They all seem to think the same, interact with typed prompts in similar ways, and overall offer very little variance from model to model. It does not matter the slight difference in word choice from model to model, or the styles in which they present them; they all seem to spout the same ideas over and over again in a predictable pattern. If we want to stay away from coming into a pattern with interacting with AI, we need to work on designing AI for more unique topics, such as water quality preservation or clean energy strategies, instead of making identical and mundane chatbots.
